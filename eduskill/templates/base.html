Our Organization

We are a non for profit organization aimed at helping children from Residential Care Homes attain tertiary and vocational educational opportunities

Our transformational Journey

These are the processes we go through to connect the Orphan child with mentors.

  • We conduct online and onsite information sessions
  • Self assessment test
  • Connecting with mentors

We conduct onsite information sessions in Residential Homes for Children/ Orpahanages. We recognize the challenge of not having access to mobile phones or computers so our model focuses on integrating both online and onsite approaches in providing information to our participants.

We conduct self assessment test to appreciate the interests and skills of participants. Based on the results, participants are given recommendations on skills they can improve on as well as educational institutions.

We connect mentors to participants based on the area of interest of the mentors. Mentors guide the participants in the entire journey of their application process. Writing motivational letters, scholarship essays, applying for transcripts and every other requirement relevant to the application.

Why Orphan Education

Lack of funding for Residential care homes

Most residential care homes do not have enough funding to help these orphans attain higher education.This leads to them ending up on the streets

Reducing poverty

When these orphans gain education and life skills, they can gain decent jobs and even create jobs

Contribution to psychological development

psychological development is very important for these orphans to provide themselves with a safe structured environment in turbulent times.
